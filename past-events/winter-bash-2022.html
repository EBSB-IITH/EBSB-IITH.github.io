Winter Bash '22

  • Conducted On: Dec 17-18, 2023

  • Photos Link(s):

In Winter Bash, we organised flurry of events and competitions. We organised tug of war in which two teams compete by pulling on opposite ends of a rope with the goal of bringing the middle of the rope over a mark on the ground, art throwback contest which was conducted in hybrid mode, Cultural Innuendo, where students can demonstrate their acting prowess or simply join in to have some fun. Taking part in competitions, and events in winters was a great fun!
